Animesh Sinha

I'm a senior staff researcher at Meta Superintelligence Labs in Menlo Park, where I work on multimodal generation.

At Meta, I have worked on large-scale machine learning systems for image and video generation, personalization, object recognition, retrieval, and representation learning at the intersection of vision and language.

I received my Master's degree in Computer Science and Statistics from Purdue University in 2018, and my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from IIT Kharagpur in 2014.
